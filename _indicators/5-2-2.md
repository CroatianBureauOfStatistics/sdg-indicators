By  measuring  the  prevalence  of  sexual  violence  by  persons  other  than  an  intimate  partner,  this  indicator  complements  the  other  priority  indicator  in  5.2  (i.e.  the  proportion  of  ever-partnered  women  and  girls  aged  15+  subjected  to  physical,  sexual  and  psychological  violence  by  a  current  or  former  intimate  partner,  in  the  last  12  months,  by  form  of  violence  and  age).
